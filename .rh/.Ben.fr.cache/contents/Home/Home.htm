The SwimClubMeet application. What is SwimClubMeet (SCM ). It's an application to manage your swimming club members and organize it's 'Club Nights'. It's not designed to do large, complex competitive meets or school swimming carnivals. It is exclusively written to run your local swimming club. Manage your members, get swimming events setup, nominate swimmers, auto-build heats, record racetimes, organise marshalls and time keepers. Get you Club's swim sessions running smoothly. It does one job, has a clean, simple interface. That's why it easy to use. Perfect for parents and friends who aren't IT experts. Start Up....
