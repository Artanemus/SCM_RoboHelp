Kick-Start Time-To-Beat (TTB).

Your first club-night.

So you have just installed SCM. Entered all your members and you are ready to run your first club night.

But you have no data! None of your swimmers have any race-times stored in the database! How will you do an Auto-Build Heats?

Firstly, auto-build will run without race data. It will fill out the heats but your nominees will be incorrectly matched. The algorithm needs race-times to create the TimeToBeat values needed to match-up swimmers.
