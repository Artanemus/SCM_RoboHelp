Training Courses in detail.

Table location and description.

Training courses are stored in the table t_TRAIN_Detail

The TrainingDetailID is an auto-number field and is created for you when you add new courses to the list. The TrainingDetailID is an important identifier that is uses later in construction of Check List items.

ShortName is the training courses name. This is used in the dropdown combobox found on the course tabsheet in training booking form.

ACCodeID stores the OBS account code.

IsArchived is described below.

All other fields are legacy or used by OBS internally and can be ignored,

How to delete a training course.

Don't remove a training course record from the t_TRAIN_Detail table as it may be ferenced by historical training bookings. Instead archive the course record by checking the IsArchived field.
